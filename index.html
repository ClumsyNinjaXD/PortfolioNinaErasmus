About Me

Nina is a final year BIS Multimedia student at the University of Pretoria. She matriculated from Highschool Dr EG Jansen in 2011 and then she moved to New York to study acting for film at the New York Film academy.

After moving back to South Africa and working for a production company, she decided that it was time to study a degree in which she would be both mentally challenged, but also be able to express herself in creative ways.

In her spare time she loves to play computer games, read novels and play with her Jack Russel, Tjoppie.
