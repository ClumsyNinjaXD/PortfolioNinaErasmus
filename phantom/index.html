About The Film

The Phantom of Pillsbury forest is a story about a young hitchhiker on his way home to his wife and children. He meets a man who tells him about a shortcut through Pillsbury forest. The hitchhiker, though hesitant, decides to enter the forest. But the forest has a few secrets of its own...
